Medical Imports

Welcome to Plyomed – Your Global Partner in Pharmaceutical Trade

At Plyomed, we specialize in the seamless export and import of pharmaceutical products, ensuring quality and reliability at every step. With a strong presence in Central and South America, including countries like El Salvador and Guatemala, we are proud to serve the global healthcare community with excellence

Our expertise spans a comprehensive range of pharmaceutical drugs, from advanced treatments in oncology and immunology to a vast selection of generic molecules for diabetes, hypertension, and more. Guided by our mission to enhance global health, we combine deep industry knowledge, robust logistics, and ethical practices to deliver unparalleled service to our partners and clients worldwide.

About

Learn More About Us

Plyomed is a team of seasoned experts dedicated to revolutionizing the pharmaceutical export and import landscape. Our multidisciplinary team includes professionals with extensive experience in manufacturing, logistics, supply chain management, sales and marketing, regulatory documentation, clinical studies, and public health. Together, we bridge the gap between pharmaceutical innovation and accessibility, ensuring that life-saving medicines reach every corner of the globe.

Our Mission
To empower global healthcare through the efficient and ethical trade of pharmaceutical products, ensuring that quality medicines reach every corner of the world. Our unwavering commitment to innovation, precision, and collaboration drives us to be the trusted partner in pharmaceutical logistics and trade.

We are committed to serving the global healthcare community with:

  • Exceptional business acumen
  • Fast and reliable delivery with guaranteed timelines
  • Ethical documentation practices
  • World-class execution from order placement to delivery to the end customer

At Plyomed, we strive to build trust and foster collaboration, ensuring that healthcare providers have access to the medications they need, when they need them.

Comprehensive Export and Import Solutions

With our expertise and dedication, we simplify the complexities of international pharmaceutical trade, allowing our clients to focus on what matters most – improving patient outcomes.

1. Pharmaceutical Drug Exports and Imports
  • Advanced therapies: Oncology, Immunology, and Biosimilars.
  • Generic molecules for chronic conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and more
  • Customizable solutions tailored to client requirements
2. Documentation and Translation Services
  • Expertise in regulatory documentation for various countries
  • Localized translations, including Spanish documentation for Latin American markets
  • In house legal support for efficient and accurate documents submission.
3. Shipping and Logistics Management
  • Streamlined shipping solutions for pharmaceuticals
  • Temperature-controlled storage and transportation for sensitive drugs
  • Efficient customs clearance to minimize delays
4. Tender Support and Local Collaborations
  • Assistance in navigating and securing tenders in Latin American countries
  • Partnership opportunities for companies entering new markets
  • End-to-end consignment execution to ensure successful delivery
