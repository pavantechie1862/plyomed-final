Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) Services

Welcome to PLYOMED – Your Trusted Partner in Infrastructure Integrity

At PLYOMED, we specialize in advanced Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) solutions to safeguard the integrity and longevity of critical infrastructure. With a strong presence in Central and South America, including countries like El Salvador and Guatemala, we are proud to support global infrastructure development with precision and expertise.

Home >

Safety

Ensuring structural integrity through continuous monitoring, early fault detection, and real-time risk assessment to prevent catastrophic failures and hazards.

Efficiency

Optimizing maintenance schedules using data-driven insights, reducing operational costs, minimizing downtime, and extending asset lifespan for sustainability.

Technology

Leveraging IoT, laser scanning, artificial intelligence, and cloud-based analytics to enhance precision, automation, and real-time decision-making processes.

OUR

SHM Services
  • Continuous Monitoring: Implementing long-term, live monitoring systems to track structural performance under various conditions.
  • Modal Analysis: Assessing the dynamic properties of structures to understand their behavior and identify potential issues.
  • Condition Assessment and Mapping: Conducting thorough evaluations to determine the current state of structures and mapping areas of concern.
  • Consultancy for Remedial Measures: Providing expert advice on structural strengthening, rehabilitation, and remedial actions to enhance performance.
  • Non-Destructive Testing (NDT): Utilizing advanced NDT techniques to evaluate structural integrity without causing damage.
  • Bridge Load Testing: Performing controlled load tests to assess bridge performance and capacity.
  • Tilt and Rotation Monitoring: Monitoring structural elements for any tilting or rotational movements that could indicate instability.
  • Scour and Water Level Monitoring: Observing and analyzing water-related factors that may affect structural foundations, especially in bridges and offshore structures.
  • Drone Inspections: Employing drone technology for efficient and comprehensive visual inspections of hard-to-reach areas.

Our Technology and Infrastructure

  • Advanced Sensors: Deployment of accelerometers, inclinometers, temperature sensors, and strain gauges for precise data collection and monitoring.
  • Data Acquisition Systems: Automated systems for real-time data collection, seamless analysis, and comprehensive reporting.
  • IoT Integration: Smart connectivity using Internet of Things devices for remote monitoring and improved decision-making.
  • Laser Scanning & GPS: High-precision tools that ensure accurate assessments and real-time structural monitoring.

Applications of SHM

  • Bridges & Tunnels: Continuous monitoring for structural integrity and safety assurance.
  • Railway Structures: Preventative maintenance and real-time diagnostics for enhanced reliability.
  • Wind Turbines: Structural health monitoring to maximize efficiency and performance.
  • Dams & Power Plants: Early fault detection to ensure long-term operational safety.
  • Historic Buildings: Preservation strategies to safeguard architectural heritage.

Benefits of SHM

  • Enhanced Safety: Early detection of structural issues prevents failures and hazards.
  • Cost Efficiency: Optimized maintenance scheduling reduces overall operational expenses.
  • Extended Lifespan: Proactive management increases the durability and longevity of assets.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Reliable insights support efficient asset management and risk mitigation.
