Plyocare PHARMACY Module

Plyocare's Electronic Medical Record (EMR) solution includes a pharmacy management feature that allows healthcare facilities to track and manage medications for their patients. This is particularly useful for hospitals, clinics, and other facilities that need to dispense and administer medications to a large number of patients.

The inventory management feature in Plyocare's EMR solution allows administrators to:

  • View and update patient medication lists
  • Setup medication reminders and alerts
  • Generate reports on medication usage and trends
  • Manage medication orders and refills

Having an accurate and up-to-date record of medications is essential for the safe and effective care of patients. This feature helps facilities ensure patients are receiving the correct doses at the right times, reducing the risk of errors and improving outcomes.

Plyocare INVENTORY Module

Plyocare's Electronic Medical Record (EMR) solution includes an inventory management feature that allows healthcare facilities to track and manage their supplies and equipment. This is particularly useful for hospitals, clinics, and other facilities that need to manage a large number of items.

The inventory management feature in Plyocare's EMR solution allows administrators to:

  • Track the quantity and location of supplies and equipment
  • Setup alerts for low inventory levels
  • Generate reports on inventory usage and trends
  • Manage vendors and suppliers
